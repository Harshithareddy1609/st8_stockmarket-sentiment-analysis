What is Stock Market Sentiment Analysis?

Stock market sentiment analysis involves evaluating the overall mood or attitude of investors towards a particular stock or the market as a whole. By analyzing news, social media, and financial reports, we can predict whether the market sentiment is positive, negative, or neutral.

Why is it Important?

Sentiment analysis helps investors make informed decisions by understanding the emotional drivers behind market movements. It provides insights into potential price changes and market trends.
